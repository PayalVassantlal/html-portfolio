Top 3 Indian Movies of 2022

1.The Kashmir Files

The film presents a fictional storyline centred around the 1990 exodus of Kashmiri Hindus from Indian-administered Kashmir.With rating of 8.3 this movie was huge boxoffice success of 2022.

2.777 Charlie

Dharma is stuck in a rut with his negative and lonely lifestyle and spends each day in the comfort of his loneliness. A pup named Charlie enters his life and gives him a new perspective towards it.If you are a animal lover like me then this movie is for you.

3.RRR

With big names like Ram Charan,NTR,Alia Bhutt,S.S.Rajamouli, this movie is a fictitious story about two legendary revolutionaries and their journey away from home before they started fighting for their country in the 1920s.Winning an Oscar for best song is not an easy job but this movie did it.
